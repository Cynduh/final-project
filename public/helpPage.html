Help & Guide

Learn how to build the perfect Pokémon deck

Searching for Cards

  1. Enter a Pokémon name in the search bar
  2. Click the Search button or press Enter
  3. Browse through the results that appear below

Adding Cards to Your Deck

  1. Find a card you want in the search results
  2. Click the 'Add' button on the card
  3. The card will appear in your deck section below

Managing Your Deck

  1. View all your deck cards in the 'My Deck' section
  2. See how many copies of each card you have
  3. Track your total card count (max 60 cards)

Removing Cards

  1. Go to the 'My Deck' section
  2. Find the card you want to remove
  3. Click the '-' button to remove one copy

Pro Tips

A competitive Pokémon TCG deck typically contains 60 cards. Try to balance your deck with Pokémon, Trainer cards, and Energy cards for the best strategy!
